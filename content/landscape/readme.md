# The Landscape

Before jumping into Azure Data Studio, it is worthwhile to step back to gaze on the big picture. There may be four ways to accomplish the same task, and in perhaps very different ways. Despite our desire to just get going, to achieve as much as possible, as quickly as possible, it helps to view your work based on shelf-life, reusability, orchestration, sharing and even collaboration.
